Welcome to the IT Club


The Information Technology (IT) Club gives students a chance to explore about numerous IT related themes outside the classroom learning to encourage a broader awareness and understanding of technology.

The IT Club is created with the intention of bridging the gap between education and industry.

The institution aims to give students a platform to exchange and develop their ideas via industry interaction, broaden their perspectives on the rapidly evolving sector of information technology.

The goal of the IT Club is to provide a setting where students may exchange information and deepen it, enhancing their technical and cognitive abilities overall.

The club also strives to foster personality development via real-world contacts and live events.

Objectives


  • Experimental experience by peer learning, by doing their hobbies which is required by learner through their creativity.
  • The trail of activities of the 2nd year student’s will be passed on to the students of 1st year automatically.
  • Provide opportunities for innovation.
  • 2 periods / week w.e.f. beginning of 3rd semester will be allocated.
  • The events may be conducted in the Conference hall and/or in the Computer center.
  • Professional activities as per the concern of the students will be entertained.
